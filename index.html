WHAT IS SHOPAWAY?

Its Simply a Solution to All Your Problems

1

Easy & Fast

Shop away is an easy and fast app designed to enable supermarkets and shops of all sizes and types to receive orders from the customers online and deliver packed items as per order at the shop counter or home. It is user friendly, affordable and easy to use.

2

More Revenue 

Customer does not have to visit the shop physically to do purchases. This helps in avoiding crowd especially in the times of pandemic, keeping customers and merchant safe. Also it saves time of both customer and the merchant. Customer can earn more revenue per square feet of the shop, compared to the conventional model..

3

Customer Friendly 

Customers can sit at the comfort of their own homes and order items in their own time. The merchant can facilitate counter pickup or home delivery as per his convenience and wish.This makes the merchant in business 24X7, since he is receiving orders even when his shop is closed.

HOW SHOPAWAY WORKS?

It Works in a Simple and Easy Way

1

Merchant Terminal

Shopaway has Two Parts. A Merchant portal and a customer app. Merchant portal is hosted safely and securely in the cloud, enabling 24X7 easy access to the merchant as well as the customers. This part is used by the merchant to receive orders from customers, queue them, fulfil them and interact with the customers on the progress of their orders via SMS.

2

Customer App

Customer app is a free app which can be downloaded from the internet through google play store. It helps the customers to register and select their favorite stores. It helps them to order their necessities online from their mobile phones or tabs with a few clicks. They can get information when their order is ready and collect it from the storefront after getting the message. If the stores have a door delivery setup, they can receive the necessities at their doorsteps.
